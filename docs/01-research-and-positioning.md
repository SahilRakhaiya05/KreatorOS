# Research and positioning

## What was researched

This v4 direction was influenced by:

- Askiva: automated research interviews, outreach, scheduling, Zoom sessions, transcripts, themes, summaries, projects/researches/participants, multilingual and timezone-aware workflows, brand-customized AI interview identity.
- Calendly: qualify, route, and schedule; routing forms; real-time website scheduling for high-value leads.
- Cal.com: workflows, triggers/actions, reminders, SMS/email, app integrations, embeddable/customizable scheduling atoms.
- Existing creator tools: bio links, storefronts, courses, coaching, memberships, paid communities, digital products, and brand workflows.

## Key insight

The winning product should not look like a generic dashboard with links. It should feel like an operating system where every object has behavior:

- A bio block is a funnel block.
- A booking type is a routing + payment + reminder workflow.
- A product is a fulfillment + customer + upsell workflow.
- A brand inquiry is a CRM + proposal + contract + payout workflow.
- A customer list is a research panel.
- An AI agent is an operator with tools, permissions, memory, and audit logs.

## Askiva-inspired translation

Askiva pattern | KreatorOS translation
--- | ---
Project | Creator workspace or brand campaign workspace
Research | Customer discovery study, brand audience study, offer validation study
Participant upload | Import customers, followers, members, buyers, brand leads
Outreach automation | Invite customers to calls, surveys, interviews, beta groups
Scheduling | Book interviews or paid calls using creator availability
AI-led interview | AI asks product/offer questions, collects objections, extracts quotes
Transcripts/themes | Customer insight board, offer recommendations, pricing objections
Brand customization | AI represents creator/brand, not the platform
Advanced edit | Fine tune tone, language, emotion, length, silence handling, guardrails

## Calendly/Cal.com-inspired translation

Scheduling pattern | KreatorOS translation
--- | ---
Routing forms | Visitor qualification before showing offers/bookings
Event types | Paid call, brand discovery, member office hours, product onboarding
Workflows | Trigger/action rules for reminders and follow-ups
Payments | Paid bookings and deposits
Embeds/atoms | Public page blocks with custom styled scheduler
Reschedule/cancel | Customer portal and automation events
Round robin/team | Agency creators, teams, coaches, support agents

## Product positioning

Not: Linktree + Calendly + Kajabi + Whop clone.

Better: AI business operator for creators.

Best: KreatorOS AI turns creator attention into a supervised business graph where AI agents create, route, sell, schedule, deliver, interview, support, and optimize every workflow.
